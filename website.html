What is Games4U?

This is a website designed to help people inform people on the biggest genres of games. Most kids will spend a lot of money on games and end up not actually playing it for a long time. I want other people, especially the younger audience, to know what they are paying for as I used to just buy games all the time but I didn't do any research about them. This is why I wasted so much money on these games.
