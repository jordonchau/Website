What are FPS Games?

FPS or First Person Shooter games are games where the player fights against other players in a first person point of view. First person shooters can be both competitive and non competitive games.

My Opinion

This is my favorite genre and one of the most popular genres of games. I love FPS games because they offer a competitive and non competitive side of gaming. You can play story mode games or fight against real people.
