What Are Strategty Games?

Strategy is a game genre that focuses on more planning and thinking rather than instant action. Most strategy games involved elements of warfare and challenge the players ability to manage an economy

My Opinion

As someone who is not very patient, I find strategy games personally very boring. However if you are someone with a lot of time and patience, the reward of winning a strategy game is definitely worth playing.

Types of Strategy Games

Some games include Age of Empires, Total War, and Sid Meier's Civilization.
