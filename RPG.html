What Are RPG Games?

RPG or Role Playing Games are games where players take on the role of fictional characters and takes on a quest in an imaginary world.

My Opinion

I personally like RPG because I find it fun to play games as a story and play as a character to beat the game. If you are someone who doesn't like story games, RPG is not for you.

Types of RPG

Some RPG are The Witcher 3, Elden Ring, and Cyberpunk 2077.
